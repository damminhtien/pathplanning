# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive community.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation harassment-free for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers via GitHub issues or repository email. Reporters are protected from retaliation. Maintainers will investigate and take appropriate action, which may include warnings or removal from the community.
